Welcome to Prosper! Our goal is to prevent Type 2 Diabetes starting from the root, throughout local low-income communities within people of color. Our team has learned that people of color are more likely to develop Type 2 Diabetes within their lifetime, due to the lack of acesss to healthy food. Thus, our target community is low-income communities that live in food desserts, where finding fresh produce is difficult to obtain and purchase. We are attempting to prevent Type 2 Diabetes, a chronic disease, because it's most prevalent in low-income communities of color.
