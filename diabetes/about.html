My name is Lizbeth Ibarra and I am an incoming sophomore at Making Waves Academy in Richmond, California. I am a 15 year old Chicana and I enjoy spending time with people that make me feel like I can be myself. Some of my hobbies include swimming, running, and playing basketball. I like to be outdoors, draw, write, and listen to music. I listen to all genres of music, but I really enjoy listening to UMI and Daniel Caesar. I also really enjoy being social because I enjoy meeting new people and making my friends laugh. This Summer, I spent most of my time at SMASH Berkeley, a a 3 year program that leads high school students through STEM careers and preparing for college. I'm not sure what I want to be when I grow up but I know that I have many interests and I'm really passionate about learning different things.
